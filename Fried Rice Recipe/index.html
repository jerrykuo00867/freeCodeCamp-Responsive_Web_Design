Fried Rice Recipe

Welcome to this guide on making fried rice. This dish is easy to prepare and does not require hard-to-find ingredients. Follow the steps below to make delicious fried rice to enjoy with friends or family.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover, cold rice)
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil or butter
  • 1 stalk green onion (sliced, optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. If using clumped cold rice, gently break it up with your hands or a spatula before cooking.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil or butter in a pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Push the garlic to one side of the pan. Pour the beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble them gently until they are mostly cooked but still slightly soft.
  4. Add the rice into the pan. Stir everything together thoroughly, breaking up any remaining rice clumps. Pour the soy sauce over the rice, then add salt and pepper. Stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes until the rice is heated through.
  5. Toss in the sliced green onions, give it a final mix, and turn off the heat. Serve hot.
